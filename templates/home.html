Welcome to the {{ project_name }}


The Aragon Outlook Digital Archive is a convenient way to search and explore historical issues of The Aragon Outlook.

The Aragon Outlook is the student-run publication of Aragon High School in San Mateo, CA. The Outlook published its first issue in 1961, the year that Aragon was founded. From 1961 until the mid-2000s it was known as The Aragon Arisocrat. Starting in 2010, the Outlook transitioned to publishing online as well as in print. All stories from the Outlook from 2010 onward can be accessed at aragonoutlook.org and thus are not included in the archive.

Acknowledgements

Former Outlook features and technology editor, Michael Herrera created the Aragon Outlook Digital Archive as a volunteer project for his senior capstone at Stanford University. Thanks to Michael Callan Landscaping for generously donating access to their diagram scanners. This allowed us to efficiently create high-quality digital scans of the physical editions. This site was created using open-oni. Thanks to the ONI team for their support in this project.
